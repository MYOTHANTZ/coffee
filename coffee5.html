Honey Spiced Latte

Perp-Time: 5minutes

Yield: One large, or two small smoothies

Diet: Honey, whipped cream, molasses, ground

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ground coffee
  • 1-1/3 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons molasses

For Finishing

Place ground coffee in the filter of a drip coffeemaker. Add water; brew according to manufacturer's instructions.

Directions:

In a small saucepan, combine the milk, honey, molasses, sugar and spices. Cook and stir over medium heat until steaming. Remove from the heat. Transfer to a blender; cover and process for 15 seconds or until foamy.
