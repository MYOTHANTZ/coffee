Whipped Banana Latte

Perp-Time: 5minutes

Yield: One large, or two small smoothies

Diet: gluten-free, vegan, dairy-free, soy-free, paleo-friendly

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup Laird Superfood Instant Mocha Latte
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 ¼ cups milk of choice
  • ½ cup ice cubes
  • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract

For Finishing

Cocoa nibs and sliced bananas (optional)

Directions:

To a high speed blender add all ingredients in the order listed. Blend until smooth, scraping the sides of the blender jar as needed with a spatula. Pour into desired glass, top with sliced bananas and cocoa nibs (if desired) and serve.
